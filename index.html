About This Guide

Pop culture tourism allows fans to explore the real-world places where their favorite movies and TV shows were filmed. This guide helps you discover those locations so you can turn fictional worlds into real travel destinations.

Additional Resources

Learn More

Ready to plan your own pop culture pilgrimage? Dive into these tools, databases, and communities tailored specifically for "set-jetters":

  • Movie-Locations.com – The ultimate database for exact street addresses and travel directions to famous film sets.
  • SetJetters – A community app built for pop culture tourists to find locations and share side-by-side photos.
  • Atlas Obscura – A global guide to hidden wonders, featuring obscure real-world locations tied to sci-fi and fantasy.
  • Geeky Explorer – A travel blog dedicated entirely to geeky destinations, offering practical itineraries for fans.
  • Fandom.com – Brush up on the deep lore, character histories, and fictional geography before you pack your bags.
  • Viator Film Tours – Book official walking tours and day trips to famous production sets all over the world.
  • IMDb – Dive deeper into cast, crew, and behind-the-scenes production trivia.
