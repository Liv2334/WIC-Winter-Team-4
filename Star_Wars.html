Filming Locations

Star Wars

Check Out These Locations if You're a Star Wars Fan

✦

Location 01

Tatooine: Tunisia & Death Valley, CA

Visit Tunisia or Death Valley in California to see where the planet of Tatooine was brought to life. The harsh desert landscapes of both locations perfectly captured the alien, sun-scorched world of Luke Skywalker's home planet.

Find on Map

Location 02

Endor: Redwood National Parks, CA

For the filming of Return of the Jedi, multiple redwood national parks in California were used — including Grizzly Creek Redwood State Park, Redwood National and State Parks in Crescent City, Jedediah Smith Redwoods State Park, and Humboldt Redwoods State Park.

Find on Map

Location 03

Kashyyyk: Phang Nga Bay, Phuket, Thailand

Visit Chewbacca's home planet in Thailand! The dramatic limestone karsts and lush tropical waters of Phang Nga Bay stood in for the Wookiee homeworld, creating one of the saga's most visually striking alien landscapes.

Find on Map

Location 04

Ahch-To: Skellig Michael, Ireland

Not only is this a UNESCO World Heritage Site, it is the island where Rey trained with Luke Skywalker in The Force Awakens. The remote, ancient stone steps and dramatic cliffs of Skellig Michael made it feel truly out of this world.

Find on Map

Location 05

Theed Royal Palace on Naboo: Palace of Caserta, Italy

Visit the Palace of Caserta for its incredible culture and walk the same grand stairways seen in the Star Wars prequels. This magnificent baroque palace served as Queen Amidala's Theed Royal Palace on the planet Naboo.
