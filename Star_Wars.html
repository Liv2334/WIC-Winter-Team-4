• Star Wars Film Locations

    Check Out These Locations if You're a Star Wars Fan!

    Tatooine: Tunisia & Death Valley, CA, USA

    Visit Tunisia or Death Valley in California, USA to see where the planet of Tatooine was imagined

    Endor: Redwood National Parks in California, USA

    For the filming of Star Wars:The Last Jedi (1983),multiple redwood national parks located in California, USA were used. Locations included: - Grizzly Creek Redwood State Park in Carlotta, California - Redwood and National State Parks in Crescent City, California - Jedediah Smith Redwoods State Park in Crescent City, California - Humboldt Redwoods State Park in Humboldt County, California

    Kashyyyk: Phang Nga Bay, Phuket, Thailand

    Visit Chewbacca's home planet in Thailand!

    Ahch-To: Skellig Michael, Ireland

    Not only is this a UNESCO heritage site, it is the island where Rey trained Luke Skywalker in Star Wars: Force Awakens

    Theed Royal Palace on Naboo: Palace of Caserta, Italy

    Visit the Palace of Caserta for its culture and walk in the same stariway as your favorite Star Wars!
