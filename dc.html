Filming Locations

DC Comics

Justice, mythology, and the silver screen

Location 01

Wayne Manor: Wollaton Hall, UK

In Christopher Nolan's *The Dark Knight Rises*, the stunning Elizabethan mansion standing in for the rebuilt Wayne Manor is Wollaton Hall in Nottingham, England. Interestingly, Nottingham is situated just north of an actual village called Gotham!

Find on Map

Location 02

Themyscira: Villa Cimbrone, Italy

To bring Wonder Woman's mythical, all-female island paradise of Themyscira to life, filmmakers utilized the stunningly picturesque Amalfi Coast in Italy, specifically leaning on the breathtaking views and ancient architecture of Villa Cimbrone in Ravello.

Find on Map

Location 03

The Joker Stairs: The Bronx, New York

One of the most instantly recognizable locations from modern comic book cinema is the steep flight of concrete stairs where Arthur Fleck dances in 2019's *Joker*. These famous steps connect Shakespeare and Anderson Avenues in the Bronx.

Find on Map

Location 04

Smallville: Plano, Illinois

For Zack Snyder's *Man of Steel*, production transformed the quiet, real-world town of Plano, Illinois into Clark Kent's hometown of Smallville, staging a massive, destructive battle between Superman and General Zod's forces right on Main Street.

Find on Map

Location 05

Wayne Enterprises: Chicago Board of Trade

The looming, art-deco skyscraper that served as the headquarters for Wayne Enterprises in *Batman Begins* is the Chicago Board of Trade Building. The streets directly below it also hosted the chaotic semi-truck flipping stunt in *The Dark Knight*.
