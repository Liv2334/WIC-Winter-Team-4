Filming Locations

Divergent

One choice can transform you

Location 01

The Choosing Ceremony: Seventeenth Church of Christ, Scientist

The iconic, brutalist-style building where Tris and the other 16-year-olds must select their factions is a real architectural marvel in downtown Chicago. Its unique circular auditorium served as the perfect setting for the tense Choosing Ceremony.

Find on Map

Location 02

Capture the Flag: Navy Pier Ferris Wheel

During the Dauntless initiation, Tris climbs a massive, rusted Ferris wheel to get a vantage point during a game of Capture the Flag. This is the famous Centennial Wheel located at Chicago's Navy Pier, visually aged using CGI for the film.

Find on Map

Location 03

The Hub: Willis Tower

Looming over the dystopian cityscape is the Hub, the center of the city's government and the highest point in Chicago. In real life, this is the Willis Tower (formerly the Sears Tower), one of the tallest buildings in the Western Hemisphere.

Location 04

Erudite Headquarters: Mansueto Library

The sleek, glass-domed building serving as the headquarters for the highly intelligent Erudite faction is actually the Joe and Rika Mansueto Library, located on the campus of the University of Chicago.

Find on Map

Location 05

Abnegation Village: Harrison & Wells Street

The identical, gray, box-like houses of the selfless Abnegation faction were physically built from scratch on a vacant dirt lot at the intersection of Harrison Street and Wells Street, right under the shadow of the Willis Tower.
