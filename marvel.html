Filming Locations

The MCU

Earth's mightiest filming locations.

Location 01

Avengers Headquarters: Porsche Experience Center

The sleek, modern facility used as the upstate New York Avengers Compound in *Captain America: Civil War*, *Spider-Man: Homecoming*, and *Avengers: Endgame* is actually the Porsche Experience Center in Atlanta, Georgia.

Find on Map

Location 02

New Asgard: St Abbs, Scotland

After the destruction of their home world, Thor and the surviving Asgardians settle in a picturesque fishing village. This quiet haven seen in *Avengers: Endgame* and *Thor: Love and Thunder* is the real-world coastal village of St Abbs in the Scottish Borders.

Find on Map

Location 03

Warrior Falls, Wakanda: Iguazu Falls

The breathtaking waterfalls where T'Challa defends his title as the Black Panther in ritual combat were heavily inspired by and augmented with establishing helicopter shots of the magnificent Iguazu Falls, located on the border of Argentina and Brazil.

Location 04

Tony Stark's Cabin: Bouckaert Farm, Georgia

The peaceful lakeside cabin where Tony Stark retires with Pepper and Morgan, and the site of his emotional memorial in *Avengers: Endgame*, is a real rental property located on the 8,000-acre Bouckaert Farm in Fairburn, Georgia.

Find on Map

Location 05

HYDRA Base, Sokovia: Fort Bard, Italy

The snowy, mountainous HYDRA research base assaulted by the Avengers in the opening sequence of *Avengers: Age of Ultron* is Forte di Bard, a massive 19th-century fortified complex in the Aosta Valley of northern Italy.
